% Shortcuts

* Seriously, learn em. Even the easy ones.

* ~Save yourself time, especially when doing repetitive tasks.~

* This applies to everything!

    Slack: ~cmd+K~
    Browser: ~cmd+T~ | ~cmd+L~ | ~cmd+opt+I~

* Editor (eg. VSCode)

    global search: ~cmd+shift+F~
    switch tabs: ~cmd+opt+arrows~
    open file: ~cmd+P~
    go-to definition: ~cmd+click~
